# LokBook Lexicon

The glossary of terms, slang, and in-universe language used across LokBook.

---

## Core Terms

| Term | Part of Speech | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **Flip** | noun | A hand-drawn animation. Short for flipbook. Each flip is a sequence of frames that play in sequence. |
| **Lok** | verb / noun | (vb.) To follow an artist or bookmark their work. "Lok an artist you love." / (n.) The in-game currency. "Earn Loks by voting, publishing, and winning battles." |
| **Lok in** | phrasal verb | To bookmark a specific piece. "I Lok'd in to that flip." |
| **Lok'd** | adjective / past tense | Having been followed or bookmarked. "She's Lok'd in with me." |
| **Loks** | noun (pl.) | The currency of the app. Used to buy cosmetics, modules, and upgrades. |
| **LilLok** | proper noun | Your living-ink companion. A creature made of ink that grows and changes based on your activity. |
| **Ink** | noun | The creative medium. Also the name for the dark stroke color. "Ink is the life of every flip." |
| **Paper** | noun | The canvas background texture. Available in plain, grid, dots, storyboard, and graphite. |
| **Blot** | noun | The default name of the LilLok. Also a type of battle intervention that obscures part of the canvas. |

## App Features

| Term | Part of Speech | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **Studio** | noun | The drawing and animation workspace. Where flips are born. |
| **Easel** | noun | The canvas rendering component within Studio. Handles layers, brushes, symmetry, and undo. |
| **Gallery** | noun | Your personal collection of published flips. |
| **Feed** | noun | The scrolling timeline of flips from artists you follow and trending content. |
| **Battle** | noun | A timed drawing competition. Players draw from the same prompt, then vote on each other's work. |
| **Rush** | noun | A fast-paced drawing challenge with random prompts and wagers. Also called Lok N Slide. |
| **Front** | noun | A Rush challenge round. |
| **Room** | noun | A shared infinite canvas for real-time collaborative drawing. Can be private or public. |
| **Drift Gallery** | noun | A public Room mode where anyone can leave one bleep per day. |
| **Bleep** | noun | A short message or mark left in a Drift Gallery. Limited to one per person per day. |
| **Viewer** | noun | The full-screen flip playback interface. Scrubs through frames or auto-plays them. |
| **Time Machine** | noun | A feature that lets you scroll through your flips chronologically. |

## Social Actions

| Term | Part of Speech | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **Vote** | verb / noun | To upvote a flip. Votes determine a flip's ranking and reward the creator. |
| **Lok** | verb | To follow an artist. See Core Terms. |
| **Lok in** | phrasal verb | To bookmark a specific flip. |
| **React** | verb | To add a reaction (Splat, Heart, or Drip) to a flip. |
| **Remix** | verb | To copy a flip's frames into Studio as a starting point for your own version. |
| **Revival** | noun | A published flip that reintroduces or reimagines a LilLok interaction. |
| **Share** | verb | To copy a flip's link or use the native share sheet. |

## Reactions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Splat** | A energized chaotic reaction. A splat of ink. |
| **Heart** | An appreciative, warm reaction. |
| **Drip** | A cool, stylish reaction. Ink dripping with style. |

## Rarities & Shop

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Common** | Base tier. Every item is available for free or a small Lok cost. |
| **Uncommon** | Slightly rarer. Slightly higher price. |
| **Rare** | Limited availability. Higher price. |
| **Epic** | Very limited. High price. |
| **Legendary** | Exclusive. Very high price. Only a few in circulation. |
| **Mythic** | The highest tier. Each Mythic item has a unique GSAP-animated preview in the shop. |
| **LokPass** | A premium pass that removes ads, unlocks all themes, and grants exclusive items. |
| **Studio UBER** | A 999 Lok bundle that unlocks every Studio module at once. |
| **Echo** | A social power-up that broadcasts a flip across the feed. |

## Visual & Technical

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Riso** | The risograph print aesthetic — two-color ink on paper. The default visual style of LokBook. |
| **Frame** | A single image in a flip's animation sequence. |
| **Page-flip** | A flip mode where frames are displayed as separate pages you scroll through. |
| **Onion skin** | A transparency overlay of adjacent frames, used as a drawing reference. |
| **Layer** | A separate drawing surface within the Easel. Layers stack on top of each other. |
| **Blend mode** | How a layer's pixels blend with the layers below. |
| **Symmetry** | A drawing tool that mirrors strokes across axes. Supports mirror X, mirror Y, 4-way, and radial. |
| **Intervention** | A random obstacle during Battle. Types: shake (canvas shakes), splat (ink splatter), blot (ink blot). |
| **Eyedrop** | A tool that picks a color from the canvas. |
| **Fill** | A tool that fills the entire layer with the selected color. |

## Lore & Flavor

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Splot** | A visual artifact or corrupted frame where the ink failed to render. "This flip came out splot." Also used as a light exclamation: "Splot!" |
| **Splot error** | When a flip cannot be displayed because its frames are missing or corrupted. The app shows "Splot error — this flip needs to be re-inked" instead of crashing. |
| **Splotched** | Adjective. Describes a flip that has visual artifacts. "That one's a bit splotched." |
| **Ink fade** | The gradual depletion of a LilLok's ink when neglected. If ink reaches zero for too long, the LilLok enters stasis. |
| **Stasis** | A dormant state. LilLok enters stasis when its ink has been at zero for more than 2 minutes. |
| **Bond** | The relationship level with your LilLok. Higher bond = slower ink drain. |
| **Ink flask** | A purchasable item that refills your LilLok's ink. |
| **Revival** | Publishing a flip from LilLok's interaction to revive it from stasis. |
| **Founder** | An early user with a backed-up account on the test server. |
| **Bleep code** | A cheat code entered in the BadBleep Box. Say "bleep" to activate. |
| **The ink** | A metaphysical force. The app's underlying logic. When something works, "the ink accepts it." |
| **LokServices** | The backend server that backs up founder data. |
